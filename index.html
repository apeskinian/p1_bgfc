About the club

What?

BGFC is a club where like minded people come along to play board games and watch films together! Most evenings there is a film showing later on so you can play some games first. Or if you don't fancy watching just carry on playing! We also have bi-monthly tournament of a chosen game, can you become the BGFC champion?

Who?

The club is open to anyone who fancies some classic fun! Pop along with friends or come and make new ones!

Where?

We meet at the local community centre. See the map below for the location

When?

The club meets up fortnightly on alternate Friday and Saturday evenings. Head over to the upcoming section to see more details of what's coming up! You can also subscribe to our newsletter to get event information straight to your inbox.

Hungry?

Feel free to bring your own food and drink, why not get a takeaway delivered to the venue!
